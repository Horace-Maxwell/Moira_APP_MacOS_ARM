# Code of Conduct

This project aims to be calm, technical, and collaborative.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Focus criticism on code, behavior, or reproducible issues.
- Share concrete context, logs, screenshots, and test steps when possible.
- Assume good intent and work toward a fix.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing private credentials or personal data
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate discussions, close threads, or remove contributions that make the project harder to collaborate on safely and productively.
